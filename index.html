Speakeasy


A Twitter-style, social media app for audio lovers where users create their own 30 second recordings to upload and share with friends. I pitched this idea, recruited two other developers, and worked collaboratively to create a functional MVP which we presented at a local tech event.

The tecnologies being used are React Native, Expo, Amazon S3, and Firebase. At the moment, Speakeasy is only available through Expo, so please first download Expo Client to demo the app.
